# Initial Concept
A simple, stress-free mobile application built specifically for my Mum to help her track her daily spending, manage recurring bills, and get clear visual insights into her finances.

# Product Definition: Mum_Finance

## Target User
- **My Mum:** The app is bespoke for her needs, focusing on ease of use and clarity.

## Core Goals
- **Stress-Free Tracking:** Provide a simple way to see daily spending and income.
- **Subscription & Bill Management:** Automatically identify and help manage recurring expenses.
- **Visual Insights:** Offer clear, visual representations of spending patterns without needing complex spreadsheets.

## Key Features
- **Effortless CSV Import:** One-click bank statement imports to eliminate manual data entry.
- **Recurring Bill Calendar:** A clear view of upcoming and past recurring bills (utilities, streaming services, etc.).
- **Smart Categorization:** Automated sorting of transactions to keep things organized without effort.

## Tone & Vibe
- **Gentle and Supportive:** The interface is designed to be non-intimidating, using simple language and a clean, helpful design to make financial management feel easy.
